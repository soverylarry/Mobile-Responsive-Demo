About Me

It's likely going to come to a shock to most folks that I, Larry Laconi, am singularly responsible for almost all of aviation's greatest achievements. Wright Brothers? Chuck Yeager? Elon Musk? Posers one and all! Through time traveling, shape shifting and generally good dental practices I've been able to leap back and forth through time with little penalty. Bending the bounds of physics, science and materials to my will is my calling card. I've never abused these powers though the temptation to persuade Buddy Holly to let me join his band was mighty tempting. So please do peruse my Portfolio knowing that I am truly a man for all times.
